Drone Physics Demo

Controls:

  • Space - Throttle (hold to go up)
  • W/S - Pitch (forward/backward)
  • A/D - Roll (left/right)
  • Q/E - Yaw (rotate left/right)
  • H - Toggle hover mode
  • R - Reset drone

Features:

  • Realistic physics with gravity and air resistance
  • Non-linear throttle response
  • Hover mode for stable flight
  • Animated propellers
